# COZY Token

COZY is the ecosystem token used within CozyVille.

## Uses

* Trading
* Marketplace transactions
* Ecosystem participation

## Why COZY Matters

Many players compare their Gold earnings against the equivalent COZY value.

## Volatility

Like all crypto assets, COZY can increase or decrease in value.

## Long-Term Perspective

Successful players often focus on growing their in-game income rather than worrying about short-term price movements.

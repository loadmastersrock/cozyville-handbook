# Beginner Guide

Welcome to CozyVille.

This guide will help you get started and avoid common mistakes.

## Your First Day

Focus on:

* Completing tutorial tasks
* Learning the map
* Understanding Gold income
* Unlocking basic activities

## Your First Week

Goals:

* Build a steady Gold income
* Learn marketplace pricing
* Upgrade key tools
* Complete daily activities consistently

## Common Mistakes

### Spending Gold Too Early

Many new players waste Gold on items that provide little value.

### Ignoring the Marketplace

Understanding prices is one of the fastest ways to improve your progress.

### Not Completing Daily Activities

Daily rewards compound over time.

## Recommended Priorities

1. Reliable income
2. Tool upgrades
3. Marketplace knowledge
4. Long-term investments

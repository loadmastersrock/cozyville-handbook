# Daily Routine

This routine is designed to maximize progression and Gold income.

## Morning

* Collect rewards
* Harvest crops
* Check marketplace prices
* List items for sale

## Afternoon

* Complete quests
* Gather resources
* Monitor profitable opportunities

## Evening

* Harvest again
* Reinvest earnings
* Prepare resources for tomorrow

## Weekly Tasks

* Review profits
* Upgrade equipment
* Track Gold production
* Review COZY exchange value

## Success Tip

Consistency is usually more important than short bursts of activity.

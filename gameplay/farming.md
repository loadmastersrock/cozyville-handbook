# Farming

Farming is one of the core activities in CozyVille.

## Getting Started

Farming allows players to grow crops and earn rewards.

## Benefits

- Earn Gold
- Gather resources
- Complete quests
- Support crafting activities

## Tips

- Check crops regularly
- Upgrade farming equipment when possible
- Focus on profitable crops as you progress

# Daily Gold Strategy

This guide focuses on maximizing your daily Gold income in CozyVille.

## Beginner Strategy

If you're new to the game:

1. Complete all daily tasks.
2. Harvest crops regularly.
3. Sell surplus resources.
4. Learn marketplace prices before making large purchases.

## Intermediate Strategy

Once established:

* Focus on the most profitable activities.
* Maintain multiple income streams.
* Track marketplace opportunities.
* Reinvest profits into upgrades.

## Advanced Strategy

Experienced players should:

* Monitor Gold to COZY exchange opportunities.
* Identify underpriced marketplace listings.
* Focus on high-value production chains.
* Optimize daily routines for efficiency.

## Tracking Progress

Keep a record of:

* Gold earned per day
* Resources produced
* Marketplace sales
* COZY value of earnings

## Example Daily Routine

Morning:

* Harvest crops
* Check marketplace

Midday:

* Complete activities
* Reinvest earnings

Evening:

* Harvest again
* List items for sale
* Review market prices

## Gold vs COZY

Many players evaluate earnings not only in Gold but also in the equivalent COZY value. Exchange rates change constantly, so review current rates regularly.

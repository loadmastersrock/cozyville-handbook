# Best Ways to Make Gold

Gold is the foundation of progression in CozyVille.

## Early Game

Focus on:

* Daily rewards
* Farming
* Resource gathering
* Quest completion

## Mid Game

Expand into:

* Marketplace trading
* Crafting
* Higher-value activities

## Late Game

Advanced players often focus on:

* Efficient production chains
* Marketplace opportunities
* Resource specialization

## Passive Income

Look for systems that continue generating value with minimal effort.

## Marketplace Flipping

Buy low and sell high.

Always understand demand before investing heavily.

## Common Mistakes

* Holding resources too long
* Buying without checking prices
* Spending all available Gold

# Gold Guide

Gold is one of the most important resources in CozyVille and is used for progression, upgrades, trading, and various in-game activities.

## What is Gold?

Gold is the primary in-game currency earned through gameplay activities.

## Ways to Earn Gold

### Farming

Growing and harvesting crops can provide a steady source of income.

### Fishing

Fishing allows players to collect valuable resources that can be sold or used elsewhere in the game.

### Mining

Mining can provide materials and resources that have value within the player economy.

### Trading

Players can buy and sell resources through the marketplace to generate profit.

### Quests and Activities

Completing tasks and daily activities often rewards Gold.

## Spending Gold

Gold can be used for:

* Equipment upgrades
* Resources
* Progression systems
* Marketplace purchases

## Tips for New Players

1. Focus on daily activities.
2. Avoid spending Gold on unnecessary items early on.
3. Learn marketplace prices before buying and selling.
4. Build multiple income sources rather than relying on a single activity.

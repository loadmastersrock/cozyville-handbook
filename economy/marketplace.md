# Marketplace Guide

The Marketplace allows players to buy and sell items with other players.

## What Can Be Traded?

Players can trade resources, materials, crafted items, and other valuable goods depending on the current game mechanics.

## Buying Items

Before buying:

* Compare prices
* Check demand
* Consider whether the item is needed immediately

## Selling Items

Successful sellers:

* Monitor market trends
* Focus on high-demand items
* Avoid panic selling

## Making Profit

Common strategies include:

* Farming resources and selling them
* Crafting items for resale
* Buying undervalued items and reselling them later

## Gold and COZY

Many players compare marketplace opportunities against the value of COZY. Market conditions can change rapidly, so always check current rates before trading.

## Tips

1. Watch prices daily.
2. Track profitable items.
3. Diversify your income sources.
4. Keep some Gold available for opportunities.

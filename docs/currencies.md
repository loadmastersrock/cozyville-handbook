# Currencies

CozyVille currently has two main currencies.

## Gold

Gold is the primary in-game currency.

### Uses

- Buying items
- Upgrading equipment
- Trading with players
- Crafting materials

## COZY

COZY is the ecosystem token used within CozyVille.

### Uses

- Marketplace transactions
- Trading
- Future game features

## Exchange Rates

Exchange rates between Gold and COZY are determined by the player marketplace and can change over time.
